Readers familiar with the classical formulation of the Birkhoff HSP theorem as an

`V-id1 : ğ’¦ âŠ« p â‰ˆÌ‡ q â†’ V ğ’¦ âŠ« p â‰ˆÌ‡ q`

This completes the formal proof of Birkhoff's variety theorem.
