### Algebraic invariants for setoid algebras

This is the [Setoid.Varieties.Invariants][] module of the [Agda Universal Algebra Library][].

Equivalently, an algebraic invariant is a predicate that factors through the isomorphism-type of `рЭС®` вАФ a property of the algebra qua structure, independent of its concrete carrier.
